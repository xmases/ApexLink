Order Lifecycle

Orders are reviewed within 24 hours. In exceptional cases, delays will be explained to the user. If accepted, payment instructions will be available. Users have 7 days to pay. If paid incorrectly, within acceptable internal error limits, the user will be contacted for refund address.

If a product is later determined to be unshippable, the full amount (including flat fee) is refunded. Refunds are processed manually via email.

Modifications and Cancellations

  • Orders can be modified by email only, to protect privacy.
  • Invalid or unsupported store links are rejected with correction suggestions.
  • Multiple valid orders per email are allowed unless flagged by internal anti-spam systems.

Payments

  • Accepted cryptos: Monero and Solana.
  • Over or underpayments outside acceptable margins will result in refund procedures via email.
  • Double payments will also be handled via refund to an address you provide.
  • Commission changes are announced on the main page and take immediate effect from the publication date. Comissions may also be charged depending on the current average market exchange fee
